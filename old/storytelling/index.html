Escape From Head Royce
Escape from Head Royce is an RPG concept written and designed by my sister, Kim Ang, and I: "An evil mormon plot is hatched to take over a private school in the Oakland hills. Without warning, teachers shed their kind exteriors and transform the Head Royce School into a dungeon of physical and psychological horror. Will our protagonists, the violent Norma Bates, and the rotund Greasy Cheeks, be able to stop them, and ride away into the sunset on an orange crate tied to a skateboard?"
