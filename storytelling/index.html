Escape From Head Royce
Escape from Head Royce is an RPG concept written and designed by me and my sister. In brief: "An evil mormon plot is hatched to take over a private school in the Oakland hills. Without warning, teachers shed their kind exteriors and transform the Head Royce School into a dungeon of physical and psychological horror. Will our protagonists, the violent Norma Bates, and the rotund Greasy Cheeks, be able to stop them, and ride away into the sunset on an orange crate tied to a skateboard?" We're hoping it'll be picked up by a AAA game design company and turned into a series with many sequels and spinoffs, and maybe a major motion picture we can star in.
Dungeons and Dragons
I've designed and drawn and mapped a few short Dungeons and Dragons campaigns. On this website, I've broken them down into modular story structures with maps, NPCs and encounter descriptions that anyone familiar with the genre can use to create their own gaming experiences. Based loosely on the Planescape setting, each story draws upon canon and non-canon material to try and engage players with interesting puzzles, and encounters, and my own flavor of humor.
NaNoWriMo
