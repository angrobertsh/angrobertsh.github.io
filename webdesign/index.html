9brag.com

9brag.com is a full stack web application that has a Ruby on Rails backend, and a React.js with Redux frontend. Modeled after our dear 9gag.com, 9brag purports to be The Feet of the Internet, the strong, large, broad memeful basis of everything good and internetty. Users collect karma by posting memes, commenting, and getting their memes upvoted in the context of a "karma is everything" environment. Read more about the technology aspects here!

CHORDEAR

A chord learning tool that lets you play the pretend piano.

More info
React, Redux
close
CHORDEAR is a web application made with a React.js and Redux frontend. Designed for aspiring musicians who'd like to improve their musical ear, CHORDEAR plays chords and expects you to play them back using your own computer keyboard! The easy and hard modes give varying levels of difficulty, from telling you what notes to play, to just playing a sound and expecting you to replicate it! A Writer's Toolkit

A text analyzer that lets you see what sorts of emotions your longer texts evoke.

More info
Plain old JavaScript
close
Instructions here. Tutorial video here.
The Writer's Toolkit is a web application made with jQuery, d3.js, and JavaScript. It visualizes the emotional word content in a piece of text, and it runs right in your browser (but not Internet Explorer [sorry]). Users input larger amounts of plain text into a text box, pick whatever emotions they'd like to see, and watch how the emotional flow of a text occurs. This project was made in 2012 under the supervision of Jon Oberlander, Micha Elsner, and Victoria Adams at the University of Edinburgh.

Colortale is the companion app to The Writer's Toolkit. Built with an elementary JavaScript coupled with the Water Colors API, this app creates artistic visualizations of sentence-by-sentence stories.
