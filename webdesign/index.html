9brag.com

My homage to the feet of the internet: memes.

 
 
React, Redux, Rails
close
9brag.com is a full stack web application that has a Ruby on Rails backend, and a React.js with Redux frontend. Modeled after our dear 9gag.com, 9brag purports to be The Feet of the Internet, the strong, broad, memeful basis of everything good and internetty. Users collect karma by posting memes, commenting, and getting their memes upvoted in the context of a "karma is everything" environment. Read more about the technology aspects here!

CHORDEAR

Learn chords and play the pretend piano.

 
 
React, Redux
close
CHORDEAR is a web application made with a React.js and Redux frontend. Designed for aspiring musicians who'd like to improve their musical ear, CHORDEAR plays chords and expects you to play them back using your own computer keyboard! The easy and hard modes give varying levels of difficulty, from telling you what notes to play, to just playing a sound and expecting you to replicate it! A Writer's Toolkit

A text analyzer that shows what emotions your words evoke.

 
 
JavaScript, jQuery, d3
close
A Writer's Toolkit is a web application made with jQuery, d3.js, and JavaScript. It visualizes the emotional word content in a piece of text with line graphs. Users input larger amounts of plain text into a text box, pick whatever emotions they'd like to see, and segment their text into sections to see the emotional flow of a text. This project was made under the supervision of Jon Oberlander, Micha Elsner, and Viccy Adams. Instructions here. Tutorial video here.

Colortale

A Writer's Toolkit but more fun.

 
 
JavaScript, jQuery, CSS
close
Colortale is the companion app to A Writer's Toolkit. Built with an elementary JavaScript coupled with the Water Colors API, this app creates artistic visualizations of sentence-by-sentence stories, changing colors and size of the lines and flowers based on the emotional words in the sentences you input. The intent of making this app is to extend the usability of the tools presented by A Writer's Toolkit. Read more about the technology aspects here!
